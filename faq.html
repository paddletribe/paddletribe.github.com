Can I learn to surf if I can’t swim?
We are passionate about introducing everyone to the exhilarating sport of surfing, no matter their swimming skills. Whether you’re not confident in the water or can’t swim at all, you can still dive into the adventure of surfing with us. Our lessons are conducted in shallow water, allowing you to always stay grounded. As long as you're comfortable being in the water, you're all set to surf.
Where Are We?
The Peddle Tribe Surf Club and campsite is located at Nirvana Beach, in the village of Kagal, Kumta.
What about Accommodation ?
At Peddle Tribe, we have partnered with Ocean Scenery Campsite, run by local people at Nirvana Beach. We provide tents for your stay, along with sleeping bags, mats, blankets, and pillows. We invite you to stay with us as we indulge in various activities, such as evening beach walks, sunset treks, and board games.
What to bring?
There are some essentials you'll need, such as a rashguard that fits your body like a second skin, shorts that are securely tied to withstand the force of the waves, hats, slippers, a water bottle, sunscreen, and any necessary medication you may require.
