How to Reach Us ?

  • Our Paddle Tribe Surf Club is located at the campsite named Ocean Scenery at Nirvana Beach. Nirvana Beach can be accessed from both Kumta and Gokarna.
  • To reach from Kumta, one can take a bus to Aghanashini or Mosalesal. Get down at the Nirvana Beach stop (Kagal Kannada Shale). From there, you can take an auto or, alternatively, you can take an auto directly to Ocean Scenery Nirvana Beach from Kumta itself.
  • From Gokarna, reach the Tadadi Port, where you can take a ferry (boat ride) to Aghanashini. From there, you can take an auto or public transportation to Nirvana Beach.
  • Note: Ferry boat carries only people and 2 wheelers.
