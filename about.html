How We Spend Our Day We are a bunch of local surfers on the shore of Nirvana Beach. We usually start our day with a light breakfast and, after staying well-hydrated, we enjoy playing games. It could be anything from frisbee to football to volleyball or even slacklining. Our morning surfing session starts soon after, and we finish it by the end of the session.

Brunch by Campsite After our surf session, brunch is something we all look forward to, as it satisfies our hunger after an active morning. We enjoy local vegetarian food from coastal Karnataka, home-cooked by our local people.

Sunset by Evening The sunset at Nirvana Beach is a highlight of every day. We have plenty of spots for watching the sunset. Nirvana Mountain, beside our campsite, is a popular sunset point. Trekking to this mountain and watching the sunset is the perfect way to end the day.

We tend to engage ourselves in different activities like fishing, crab hunting, or trekking to various spots nearby, such as Nirvana Mountain or the Aghanashini River. Sometimes, we enjoy a beach walk at night. Our whole motive is to showcase the local surfing lifestyle to anyone willing to join and experience our way of life.
