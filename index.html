Hello there! We are a group of surfers who started this club at Nirvana Beach. We're all about a laid-back, slow, and chill atmosphere while enjoying surfing and other activities. We want to share this experience with people who are willing to enjoy these things with us, so I welcome you to Pedal Trib Surf Club.

We started out as curious people about learning this new activity. I'm Govind Birkodi, and I remember the first day I got introduced to surfing by my cousin. After that, there was no turning back. My friend, Sachin SR, who tagged along as a stoked surfer, joined me. We've been enjoying this laid-back, healthy lifestyle that feels complete in itself. We wanted to share our lifestyle with people who are willing to enjoy it with us.

We aim to raise ocean awareness by engaging people in watersports and promoting safety. Our focus is on showcasing a healthy, slow-paced lifestyle rooted in nature. We also want to share our local culture, traditions, and food, offering a glimpse into life by the sea. Through these experiences, we hope to inspire others to connect with the ocean in a mindful and respectful way.

Our surf club is located at Nirvana Beach in the village of Kagal, between Gokarna and Kumta. We're partnered with a local campsite called Ocean Scenery. The stunning Kagal Mountain is to our left, the Aganeshini River runs beside us, and the Arabian Sea is right in front.
